# COST & AFFORDABILITY STRATEGY

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Pickett Applied Technologies Laboratories, LLC (PAT Labs) presents a realistic, defensible cost strategy for the Dragon Scale thermal runaway mitigation solution, leveraging our lean SDVOSB structure and ISO 9001/27001 certified processes to deliver exceptional value. Our $1,850,000 Firm Fixed Price OT prototype proposal reflects a disciplined approach to cost estimation, with 95% of resources dedicated to direct technical execution and validation.

## 2. COST REALISM & AFFORDABILITY POSITIONING

### 2.1 Lean Execution Advantages
- **Minimal Overhead Structure**: As a founder-led SDVOSB with no full-time staff or facility leases, we maintain overhead at <10% compared to industry averages of 25-40%, directly reducing government costs.
- **ISO-Certified Efficiency**: Our ISO 9001:2015 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certified processes ensure disciplined execution without the bloat of larger organizations, providing auditable quality and security controls.
- **Direct Technical Focus**: 95% of proposed costs fund direct technical work, materials, and validation testing, maximizing the government's investment in capability development.

### 2.3 Affordability Discriminators
- **Copper-Free Design**: Eliminates dependency on critical materials with volatile pricing and supply chain risks.
- **Scalable Manufacturing**: Bench-scale processes use commercially available equipment, enabling low-rate initial production without major capital investment.
- **Dual-Use Commercialization**: Development costs are offset by commercial EV market potential, reducing future military production costs.
