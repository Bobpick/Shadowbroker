# Cost & Affordability Strategy

## Lean Execution Advantages as Direct Government Benefits

As a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) with ISO 9001:2015 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certifications, PAT Labs offers several unique cost advantages that directly benefit the government:

### 1. Minimal Overhead Burden
- **Lean Structure**: As a founder-led SDVOSB with minimal overhead (W9128Z-25-S-A002), we can deliver high-value solutions without the typical prime contractor burden of large PM offices, extensive compliance teams, or multiple layers of management.
- **Direct Technical Accountability**: Our ISO-certified quality system ensures rigorous execution while maintaining a lean organizational footprint.

### 2. Rapid Response & Agility
- **ISO Quality System**: Our certified quality management systems (W9128Z-25-S-A002) enable rapid, disciplined response to changing requirements without compromising quality.
- **Founder-Led Development**: Direct technical accountability through our founder's hands-on involvement ensures quick decision-making and agile adaptation.

### 3. Domestic Supply Chain & Risk Reduction
- **Domestic Materials**: Our technologies use domestically sourced feedstocks (W9128Z-25-S-A002), reducing supply chain vulnerabilities.
- **Reduced SWaP-C Penalties**: By eliminating copper wiring and steel structures, we reduce logistics burden while improving reliability.

## Affordability Positioning

Our affordability positioning is grounded in:

1. **Lean Execution Model**: Our ISO-certified quality system enables efficient execution without the overhead burden of larger primes.
2. **Domestic Supply Chain**: Using domestic materials reduces long-term logistics costs and supply chain vulnerabilities.
3. **Reduced SWaP-C Penalties**: The 60-80% reduction in weight versus legacy solutions (W9128Z-25-S-A002) directly translates to lower transportation, maintenance, and deployment costs.
